More Information about the art site

What is this website ?

This website showcases student artwork from our art class. It displays paintings in different styles like Acrylic, Watercolor, and Ink. The website celebrates student creativity and gives young artists recognition for their hard work.

Where is this art works from?

All the artwork displayed on this website comes from students in our art class. Each painting was created by talented young artists using different techniques and styles. With their permission, we collected their work and organized it online to share with the school community.
